THE LAST STRAW

Who are we?

The Last Straw is a student-led campaign dedicated to eliminating the use of plastic straws in the University District. We want to promote the use of alternatives to plastic straws in order to do what we can to help our environment. Although we are currently a smaller organization, we believe every bit of help can contribute greatly to our environment as our message will be spread to more people that could potentially create their own organizations that work towards using less plastic.
